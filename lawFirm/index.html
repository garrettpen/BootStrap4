Trust Preparation

The Revocable Living Trust is the heart of an excellent estate plan for you and your loved ones. This instrument allows you to be in charge of how your estate will be handled in your absence.
Key Features:

  • Avoid Probate—probating an estate is expensive and time consuming.
  • Know who will take care of your children and how they will be taken care of.
  • Know how and when and to whom your property will be dispersed. Important financial information is in one place
  • Key individuals are in place to handle your estate exactly as you intend it to be handled.

Will Preparation

The Will or “Pour Over” Will serves the Revocable Living Trust by ensuring that any property that was not placed into the Trust, is owned by the Trust anyway. This way in case you make a mistake in the future, your estate plan is still intact

Healthcare Directive

Power Of Attorney

This document ensures that your financial well being is taken care of by a spouse, relative or trusted friend after you are unable to take care of your finances. This document also relieves a tremendous amount of stress from family since financial institutions and others will not allow your finances to be handled by anyone but you without your permission. This document provides that permission.
